About

Hello! My name is Caroline Love, and I am the creator of MusicMatcher. I created this forum to allow users to give each other song suggestions, or share their playlists from Spotify or other music platforms with others. If you are like me and get sick of listening to the same songs over again, and you don't know where to begin in finding new music, this platform is for you! If you are curious as to why there are only four genres in the navigation menu, it's because I initially intended to look for music that caters to my tastes and take your suggestions from each genre, but feel free to post any song recommendations or playlists in one of the four available genres, and I may add more genres as this site gains more traction.
